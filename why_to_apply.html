Why to Apply Individual Health Insurance?

Today, medical expenses are escalating and counting heavily on the pockets of people. Health insurance helps in covering unexpected expenditure caused by any sudden medical emergency.


Currently, majority of the organizations provide health cover to their employees which offers security to most of the employees to cover such health emergencies. However, they often do not realize needof personal health insurance anditsbenefits. It is strongly advised that professionals should opt for personal health insurance cover along with the health insurance provided by the companies. The following points signify the importance of personal health Insurance.

Do you have questions?

Points You Need to Know

  • Cashless service is available only at the network hospitals.
  • If the request for cashless settlement is denied, you can still pay for the expenses at the hospital from your pocket and then request for reimbursement.
  • To avail the cashless facility, it is important to send a timely intimation to the insurance company/TPA.
  • If the total cost of treatment exceeds the initial approved amount, the hospital can request the TPA or the insurance company to enhance this amount as per the expenses.
  • All the necessary documentation, prescriptions and doctorâ€™s reports should be furnished, if required by the insurance company/TPA.
  • Always share a copy of your health insurance documents and identity card with your family/friends/relative for timely use. It is mandatory to display the health insurance card at the time of admission in the hospital to avail cashless settlement.
  • Cashless settlement of claims is purely subject to terms and conditions mentioned in the policy wordings.

Insurance in Effect

The validity of the health insurance provided by your organization is limited. It is only on roll until the time you are serving in that organization. One could also opt for a personal health insurance, to utilize various related benefits in case of potential job change, job loss or a retirement.

Stress-free Funding

Securing oneself with individual health insurance policy ensures that it will be easy for you to pay your medical bills. The health insurance also prevents you from raising/ arrangingfunds at a short notice.

No Compromise on Plans

Having a health insurance ensures that sudden medical expenses wouldnot make you compromise with the plans that you might have with your savings.

Instant Treatment

Being insured ensures that the treatment required will be taken ahead on time to the enlisted medical institutions. Whereas, inthe absence of insurance, there could be a delay in findingthe right place for treatment in right time.

Preventive Check-ups

Insurance acts as a stimulator to get various health check-ups done at regular intervals. This helps you identify problems before they turn into a serious medical condition.

Assurance

The confirmation, that the expenses/expenditures for your hospitalization or treatment will be taken care of easily, allows you to recover in a better frame of mind rather than worrying about the funding of treatment.

Access to Varied Available Resources

Health insurance coverage offers you access to a wider range of resources like specialist doctors, hospitals and treatments. An insured person also has the advantage of quicker treatment than a person not having any kind of health insurance.

Multiple Choices Offered

Numerous types of individual health insurance policies are available in the market and you can choose the one which suit your needs. Many insurance companies also offer to provide customized health plans to you.

Starting Early

At young age, when one is free from any medical complications its cost efficient tobuy a personal health insurance policy. As the premium is lower and the policy offers comprehensive coverage in comparison to a policy purchased at an older age.

Tax Benefit

One of the major benefits of having a personal health insurance policy is that you getthe tax benefit. Payments made towards health insurance premiums are eligible for tax deductions under section 80D of the Indian Income Tax Act.
