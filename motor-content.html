For more than a decade, motor insurance has been a beneficial investment option for safeguarding your two- and four-wheelers. The reason for such prudent investments is to acquire aid against damages during road incidents. You can buy vehicle insurance policies online, which is a necessity in India as the traffic here is not that generous towards fellow automobilists.

A Brief Introduction to Various Motor Insurances

Here is a list of popular vehicle insurance policies

Car Insurance

With this policy, you can avail accidental benefits not only for your own car but also for some other party. Whenever you look forward to choosing a protection policy for your vehicle, make sure that you have checked and compared all the investment offers online. Furthermore, the invested amount in the protection plan also depends on the value of your car, year of manufacturing and registration date.

Two Wheeler Insurance

You can also buy vehicle policies online for two-wheelers such as scooters and bikes. The benefits for this category are quite similar to that for a car.

Commercial Vehicle Insurance

This particular policy enables commercial vehicle drivers to obtain benefits for damages to their industrial vehicles. Such vehicles include the ones that are meant for carrying goods for business purposes.

Third-party Insurance Plan

Many times, someone gets injured in an accident that may involve your car. While the benefit does not directly benefit the one insured, it does help in problematic situations involving unfortunate accidents with others.

Comprehensive Insurance Cover

This additional benefit to the third-party insurance policy helps take care of all the expenses dealing with an accident involving some other person. It not only provides coverage for the person but also to you.

Liability only Plan

This too is an add-on to the third party policy where any property damage and injuries dealt during an accident are covered. It also ensures accidental coverage for the driver.

Included & Excluded

What is all included in a motor insurance policy?

A number of motor insurance companies in India provide policies that cover up damages due to the following:

  • Natural calamities like cyclone, storm, earthquake, floods, landslides, etc.
  • Burglary and fire.
  • Strike and riots.

What is all excluded from a vehicle insurance policy?

Do remember that the motor insurance online or otherwise is not liable for coverage in below incidents:

  • Any damage to the vehicle outside India is not liable for coverage.
  • Invalid driver's license.
  • If the vehicle is serving an illegal purpose or activity.
  • If the driver is under an influence of alcohol or drugs.

Why is there a need for motor insurance?

Taking a look at the unpromising road conditions in India, it becomes quite necessary to opt for motor vehicle insurance. Such plans offer a variety of financial coverage that benefits both you and other parties damaged in an incident. Moreover, a motor insurance policy purchased through a private insurance provider covers up far more benefits than a government one. Some of these benefits are:

  • Facility of towing
  • Assistance for road side incidents, 24/7
  • Coverage for engine protection
  • Coverage for depreciation
  • Network garage claims either through cashless coverage or direct agreements

How can motor insurance be claimed?

As stated earlier, the formalities and documents that are dealt with during the settlement of a vehicle insurance claim are based on the nature of the damage and the type of vehicle involved.

Claiming for damage to owned vehicle

For this settlement, there is a need to provide an estimate of the damages in detail to the insurer. After that, a team of surveyors from an engineering trade is given the responsibility of checking the damages and the reasons behind it. After full and detailed inspection, these surveyors submit their report to the company, which further examines it. An authorized letter is then issued for repairs.
Besides the claim form, there are other documents needed in the process as well. These documents are:

  • Police report
  • The final bill issued by the repairers
  • Registration Certificate Book
  • Driver's license
  • Fitness certificate (if it is a commercial vehicle)

Third-party claims

Once the notice is received from the third party or insured, the issue is then taken to an advocate. The advocate then takes all the necessary documents from the one insured including the details of the accident. The documents are:

  • Death certificate when there is fatal loss claim involved.
  • Medical certificate
  • Driver's license
  • Police report

Guide to reduce Premium Rates for Vehicle Insurance

To reduce premium rates, you can try the following tips that will ensure you profitable investment plans for your motor insurance policies.

  • Keep track of the discounts: Most insured people do not know that there are many other small, but advantageous bonuses and discounts except the popular no-claim ones. There have been rewards and benefits for various policies where the claim history is good enough. So, it is prudent to keep track of such discounts.
  • Use of anti-theft devices: If you install such devices that are approved by Automobile Research Association of India, then you can claim for additional discounts in your premium rates.
  • Avoiding adding additional drivers: Adding more drivers to the online vehicle insurance plan will only increase the premium rates. So, it is better to avoid if the drivers are already having life insurance coverage of their own.
  • Always compare: This is one of the most important tips that will give you the choice to choose better offers and make your purchase accordingly. Look up for a beneficial insurance plan to keep you safe on the road.
